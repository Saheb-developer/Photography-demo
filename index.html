About Us

BSG Pictures

BSG Pictures is a team of passionate photographers and filmmakers based in Kathmandu, Nepal. With a keen eye for detail and a love for storytelling, we capture the essence of every moment — from the nervous excitement of a bride to the candid laughter of a portrait session.

Our style blends documentary authenticity with fine-art aesthetics, ensuring your memories are preserved as beautifully as they were lived. We serve clients across Nepal, including Kathmandu, Pokhara, Chitwan, and beyond.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend at least 2–3 months in advance for bride photoshoots and pre-wedding shoots. For weddings, 4–6 months is ideal.

How long does it take to get photos?

Portrait sessions: 1 week. Bride shoots: 2 weeks. Weddings: 2–3 weeks for photos, 4–6 weeks for cinematic films.

Do you travel outside Kathmandu?

Yes! We serve all of Nepal. Travel costs may apply for destinations beyond the valley.

Can I customize a package?

Absolutely. We tailor every shoot to your unique style, location, and budget. Just contact us with your requirements.
